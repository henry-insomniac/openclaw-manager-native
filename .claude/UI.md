# UI North Star

## Product Role

`OpenClaw Manager Native` is not a generic dashboard.

Its job is to make local OpenClaw maintenance understandable and executable for users who do not want to debug with shell commands.

## Primary Promise

- Make local state visible
- Turn maintenance into safe buttons
- Reduce the need for Terminal knowledge

## UX Priorities

1. Lead with the current judgment
2. Show the first safe action clearly
3. Keep technical detail available, but secondary
4. Prefer progressive disclosure over dumping every signal at once
5. Keep labels and buttons specific: say what will happen

## Visual Direction

- Dark utility console
- Calm, precise, not flashy
- Strong hierarchy through spacing, weight, and contrast
- Fewer nested cards
- Better scanability in narrow and wide window sizes

## Copy Direction

- Chinese-first
- Short sentences
- No slogan-style fluff
- No repeated restatement of the same idea
- Errors should answer: what happened, why it matters, what to do next
